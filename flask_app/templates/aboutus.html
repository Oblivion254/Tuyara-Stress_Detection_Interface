About Us



Welcome to our Stress Detection Interface Project!


At Tuyara, we are dedicated to revolutionizing the way individuals manage their stress levels by harnessing the power of technology. Our team has come together with a shared mission: to create an innovative and accessible solution that empowers users to take control of their mental well-being.

With the prevalence of stress-related disorders on the rise, we recognized the urgent need for a proactive approach to stress management. Leveraging cutting-edge artificial intelligence and image processing techniques, our interface provides users with real-time insights into their stress levels based on subtle cues in facial expressions.


But our project is about more than just technology. It's about making a meaningful impact on people's lives. We understand that stress affects everyone differently, which is why our interface is designed to be intuitive, customizable, and user-friendly. Whether you're a busy professional, a student juggling multiple responsibilities, or simply someone looking to improve their overall well-being, our interface is here to support you on your journey to a calmer, more balanced life.

As we continue to refine and enhance our stress detection interface, we invite you to join us on this exciting journey. Together, we can unlock new possibilities for mental wellness and create a brighter, healthier future for all.


Thank you for being a part of our community.
